GheeRice And ButterChicken

Ghee Rice is a fragrant and flavorful Indian dish made by cooking basmati rice in aromatic ghee (clarified butter) along with spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. The result is a rich, buttery rice that perfectly complements a variety of curries or grilled meats. Butter Chicken,or Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish featuring tender, marinated chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce rich with butter, cream, and spices. It has a mildly spiced, velvety sauce that pairs beautifully with naan or ghee rice.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice – 2 cups
  • Ghee (clarified butter) – 2-3 tablespoons
  • Yogurt – 1/2 cup (for marination)
  • Boneless chicken thighs/breasts – 500 grams, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Red chili powder – 1 teaspoon (for marination)
  • Cashew paste – 2 tablespoons (soak and blend cashews)
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) – 1 teaspoon
  • Honey or sugar – 1 tablespoon (optional, for sweetness
  • Garam masala – 1 teaspoon (for marination)
  • Fresh coriander – for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt then add the boneless chicken pieces and coat well and marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight
  2. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat and add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes and remove from the skillet and set aside
  3. In the same skillet, add a bit more butter if needed and sauté onion until soft and golden brown then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens then add cashew paste, red chili powder, garam masala, kasuri methi, and salt and cook for another 5 minutes
  5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and mix well with the sauce then stir in cream and honey or sugar if using and cook for an additional 5 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with ghee rice or naan bread

Enjoy your flavorful ghee rice paired with rich and creamy butter chicken!
